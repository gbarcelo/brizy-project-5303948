Our Story

Collaboration and growth.

We were first brought together through a common interest in the effective negotiation techniques of Chris Voss and his Masterclass on negotiation. Though Chris refined his skills in the high-stakes world of hostage negotiation, we as classmates understand the applicability of these skills to almost every aspect of life, not limited to work, family, friends, and even daily interactions with a local barista or store clerk. Our interests have expanded to a variety of other negotiation and communication techniques and concepts which we use in both original and innovative ways.

One thing was clear – we could not gain these skills by simply watching videos. Practice is essential. So, we started this group in order to facilitate practice sessions. Although we do not serve as educators or trainers, our platform is like a gym where people from all over the world come to “work out” and sharpen their skills.
